I'm Joburg Ash

Born and raised in Soweto, I'm a third-generation grower turned urban farmer and artist. I build living installations that make food security visible, beautiful, and cultural.

Through Jozi Food Farmer (JFF), I've transformed 14+ rooftops across Johannesburg into thriving urban farms. From Braamfontein to Hillbrow, Newtown to Troyeville, we're proving that cities can feed themselves—one rooftop at a time.
